Husn Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

At Husn (https://husn.app), we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication and communication.
  • Name: Used for personalization and display.
  • Google Profile Picture URL: Used for display purposes within the app.
  • Search Queries: Used to improve search results and provide personalized recommendations.

Cookies

We use cookies for authentication purposes and to store your access token. These cookies are essential for the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Sharing

We share anonymized, aggregated data with Google Analytics to analyze website usage and improve our services. This data cannot be linked back to individual users.

User Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data. Please contact us through our Instagram page (instagram.com/husn.app) to request data deletion.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Instagram page (instagram.com/husn.app).
